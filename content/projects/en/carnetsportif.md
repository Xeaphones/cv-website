  - "Use of Figma in association with a UI/UX designer to renew the site's identity and make it more accessible for any user; Figma allowing a simplified integration of the frontend thanks to a model made in advance."
  - "Use of a backend in Go, serving as an API server, serving data on users in order to have information such as their sessions, their records or others."

I would also like to thank the entire team with whom I worked but also my internship supervisor for this more than enriching experience."
